Audiopig Support

Audiopig is a local audiobook player for iOS. Import MP3 and M4B files, listen with full player controls, and keep your library on your device — no account required.

Contact

Questions, feedback, or privacy requests: audiopigsupport@gmail.com

Common questions

  • Import audiobooks: Library tab → + → Import Files or Import Folder (MP3 / M4B, non-DRM).
  • Restore purchases: Settings → Audiopig Plus → Restore Purchases.
  • Manage subscription: Settings → Manage Subscription, or your Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Privacy: Audiopig does not collect personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
